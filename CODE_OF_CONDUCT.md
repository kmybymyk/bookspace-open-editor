# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

BookSpace Open Editor should be a practical, respectful project for people
working on EPUB authoring and web editing tools.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct and respectful.
- Keep criticism technical and specific.
- Assume public project discussions may be read by contributors with different
  backgrounds and experience levels.
- Avoid harassment, insults, or personal attacks.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated off-topic disruption
- Abusive comments about a person rather than their work

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this
code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or
permanent loss of participation privileges in the project.

Report conduct concerns to the repository maintainers through GitHub.
